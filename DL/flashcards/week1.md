If you remove all nonlinear activations (ReLU, sigmoid, etc.) from a deep neural network but keep multiple fully connected layers, what happens?
**A.** It still works as a universal approximator.
**B.** It becomes equivalent to logistic regression.
**C.** It reduces to a single linear transformation, losing expressive power.
**D.** It will learn faster because no nonlinearity is present.
?
C. It reduces to a single linear transformation, losing expressive power
